The Frugalware Developer Team is pleased to [announce](/news/43) the immediate availability of Frugalware 0.5rc2, the second release candidate of the upcoming 0.5 stable release.  

 A short and incomplete list of changes since 0.5rc1:  

* Updated GNOME to 2.16 Release Candidate (2.15.92)
* ~300 bugfixes
* Updated KDE and GRUB artwork
* More than 30 new packages: ntfs-3g, avifile, gnome-sharp and much more!


 Please refer to the Frugalware [Testing ChangeLog](http://ftp.frugalware.org/pub/frugalware/frugalware-testing/ChangeLog.txt) for more information.
