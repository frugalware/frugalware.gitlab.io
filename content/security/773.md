Daniel Kahn Gillmor discovered that GnuPG treated keys with empty usage flags as being valid for all usages. (CVE-2013-4351)
 Taylor R Campbell discovered that GnuPG incorrectly handled certain OpenPGP messages. (CVE-2013-4402)
 Daniel Genkin, Adi Shamir, and Eran Tromer discovered that GnuPG was susceptible to an adaptive chosen ciphertext attack via acoustic emanations. (CVE-2013-4576)
